1. What Chama Fácil is — and is not

Chama Fácil is a platform that connects people who need a service to independent professionals who perform it. We broker the match and provide the tools for quoting, tracking, payment and rating, and we verify everyone who signs up as a professional.

We do not perform the services. The service is provided directly by the professional, who is not our employee, agent or representative. There is no employment relationship between Chama Fácil and the professionals on the platform.

4. Rules for professionals

  • Sign-up goes through verification: ID document, proof of address and a selfie. Only approved professionals receive requests.

5. Rules for customers

  • Describe the job and the location accurately: a good quote depends on good information.
  • Make sure the professional can access the location at the agreed time.
  • Pay the agreed amount through the chosen method once the job is done.
  • Rate honestly. Ratings go both ways and are what keeps quality up.

6. Prices, payment and fees

The customer states a budget and receives quotes; the final price is the one in the accepted quote. Payment happens after completion, by Pix, card or cash, depending on the options available.

During early access, professionals pay no subscription and no commission. When plans and commissions come into force, we will give notice in advance and you may close your account if you disagree.

7. Cancellation and no-shows

  • If the professional does not show up, report it through the app: the request is reopened at no cost to you and the case is reviewed.
  • Any specific cancellation fees or penalties, where they apply, will be shown in the app before you confirm.

8. When something goes wrong

If a job goes wrong — poor work, damage, an incorrect charge — open a case through the app within 7 days of completion. Our team mediates based on the request history, the messages and the before-and-after photos.

Within that window you can ask for the job to be redone or for a refund.

The professional who performed the job is the one liable for damage. Chama Fácil does not perform the service and is not an insurer: what it does is verify who shows up, keep the evidence of the request and mediate. Carrying insurance is each professional's own choice and is not required by the platform — where it exists, it is self-declared.
